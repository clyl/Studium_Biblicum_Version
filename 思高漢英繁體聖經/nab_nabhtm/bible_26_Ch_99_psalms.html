聖詠集:Chapter 99

聖詠集 Psalms
1上主為王，萬民因而戰慄驚恐，他坐於革魯賓之上，大地震動； 1 The LORD is king, the peoples tremble; God is enthroned on the cherubim, the earth quakes.
2熙雍的上主，偉大堂皇，崇高尊貴，而超越萬邦。 2The LORD is great on Zion, exalted above all the peoples.
3願他們讚美你的大名，它可敬可畏，至尊神聖。 3Let them praise your great and awesome name: Holy is God!
4你是愛正義的大能君王，是你制定了法律的正綱，對雅各伯行的合理合章。 4O mighty king, lover of justice, you alone have established fairness; you have created just rule in Jacob.
5請你們尊崇上主，我們的天主，還要向着他的腳凳伏地叩首。因為他的腳凳也是神聖無偶。 5 Exalt the LORD, our God; bow down before his footstool; holy is God!
6梅瑟和亞郎列於上主的司祭中，撒慕爾屬於呼號他聖名的人中，他們呼號上主，上主即俯聽他們。 6Moses and Aaron were among his priests, Samuel among those who called on God's name; they called on the LORD, who answered them.
7他從前曾在雲柱中訓示了他們，他們就守了他吩咐的誡命章程。 7From the pillar of cloud God spoke to them; they kept the decrees, the law they received.
8上主，你原是我們的天主，你曾俯聽了他們，天主，你寬宥他們，但也報復了他們的惡行。 8O LORD, our God, you answered them; you were a forgiving God, though you punished their offenses.
9請你們尊崇上主，我們的天主，還要向着他的聖山伏地叩首，因我們的天主，上主神聖無偶。 9Exalt the LORD, our God; bow down before his holy mountain; holy is the LORD, our God.
