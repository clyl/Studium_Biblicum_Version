列王紀上 1 Kings
1撒羅滿王作王統治全以色列。 1Solomon was king over all Israel,
2以下是他所有的官吏：匝多克的兒子阿匝黎雅作大司祭， 2and these were the officials he had in his service: Azariah, son of Zadok, priest;
3史沙的兩個兒子厄里曷勒夫和阿希雅作書記，阿希路得的兒子約沙法特作御史， 3Elihoreph and Ahijah, sons of Shisha, scribes; Jehoshaphat, son of Ahilud, chancellor;
4約雅達的兒子貝納雅統率軍隊，【匝多克和厄貝雅塔爾為司祭，】 4(Benaiah, son of Jehoiada, commander of the army; Zadok and Abiathar, priests;)
5納堂的兒子阿匝黎雅為太守之長，納堂的兒子匝步得為君王的朋友， 5Azariah, son of Nathan, chief of the commissaries; Zabud, son of Nathan, companion to the king;
6阿希沙爾為家宰，阿貝達的兒子阿多尼蘭管理苦役。 6Ahishar, major-domo of the palace; and Adoniram, son of Abda, superintendent of the forced labor.
7撒羅滿另立了十二個太守管理全以色列，供應君王和王室的食糧；一年之中每人供應一個月。 7 Solomon had twelve commissaries for all Israel who supplied food for the king and his household, each having to provide for one month in the year.
8他們的名字如下：胡爾的兒子······管理厄弗辣因山地； 8Their names were: the son of Hur in the hill country of Ephraim;
9德刻爾的兒子······管理瑪卡茲、沙耳賓、貝特舍默士、阿雅隆及貝特哈南； 9the son of Deker in Makaz, Shaalbim, Beth-shemesh, Elon and Beth-hanan;
10赫色得的兒子······管理阿魯波特、索苛及赫斐爾全地； 10the son of Hesed in Arubboth, as well as in Socoh and the whole region of Hepher;
11阿彼納達布的兒子······管理多爾全境，他娶了撒羅滿的女兒塔法特為妻； 11the son of Abinadab, who was married to Solomon's daughter Taphath, in all the Naphath-dor;
12阿希路得的兒子巴阿納，管理塔納客、默基多直到約刻默罕外，以及位於依次勒耳下邊的全貝特商，從貝特商直到靠近匝爾堂的阿貝耳默曷拉； 12Baana, son of Ahilud, in Taanach and Megiddo, and beyond Jokmeam, and in all Beth-shean, and in the country around Zarethan below Jezreel from Beth-shean to Abel-meholah;
13革貝爾的兒子······管理辣摩特基肋阿得，和在基肋阿得地的默納協的兒子雅依爾的鄉村；在巴商、阿爾哥布地，所有的六十座設有城牆和銅閂的大城市，也歸他管理； 13the son of Geber in Ramoth-gilead, having charge of the villages of Jair, son of Manasseh, in Gilead; and of the district of Argob in Bashan--sixty large walled cities with gates barred with bronze;
14依多的兒子阿希納達布管理瑪哈納因； 14Ahinadab, son of Iddo, in Mahanaim;
15阿希瑪茲管理納斐塔里，他也娶了撒羅滿的女兒巴色瑪特為妻； 15Ahimaaz, who was married to Basemath, another daughter of Solomon, in Naphtali;
16胡瑟的兒子巴阿納管理阿協爾和則步隆； 16Baana, son of Hushai, in Asher and along the rocky coast;
17帕魯亞的兒子約沙法特管理依撒加爾； 17Jehoshaphat, son of Paruah, in Issachar;
18厄拉的兒子史米管理本雅明； 18Shimei, son of Ela, in Benjamin;
19烏黎的兒子革貝爾管理加得，即昔日屬阿摩黎王息紅和巴商王敖格的地區。猶大地區另派太守管理。 19 Geber, son of Uri, in the land of Gilead, the land of Sihon, king of the Amorites, and of Og, king of Bashan. There was one prefect besides, in the king's own land.
20當時猶大和以色列人數，像海邊的沙粒那麼多；他們有吃有喝，非常快樂。 20Judah and Israel were as numerous as the sands by the sea; they ate and drank and made merry.
